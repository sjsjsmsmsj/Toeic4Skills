Part 1:
--- Cần đáp ứng các yêu cầu có đủ hai từ trong câu, đúng ngữ pháp, mạch lạc.
1. Có thể viết một trong các cách: 
--- Câu hỏi đưa hai từ gợi ý: Toy/ Shelf
- There are many/ a lot of toys displayed on the shelves.
- There are different kinds of toys displayed on the shelves.
- A lot of toys are displayed on the shelves.
2. Có thể viết một trong các cách: 
------- Câu hỏi đưa hai từ gợi ý: carry/ box
- The woman is carrying a big box.
- The woman in a polka-dot shirt is carrying a box.
3. Có thể viết một trong các cách: 
------- Câu hỏi đưa hai từ gợi ý: woman / in
- The woman is making a phone call in her car.
---- To make a phone call = to call
     To receive a phone call
     To have a phone call
4. Có thể viết một trong các cách: 
--------Câu hỏi đưa hai từ gợi ý: check/ because
------ S1 + V1 because S2 + V2
- The man is checking a map because he is lost in the forest.
- The man is checking a map because cannot find the way.
5. Có thể viết một trong các cách:
------- Câu hỏi đưa hai từ gợi ý: take / off
- The airplane is about to take off.
- The plane is on the runway ready to take off.
- There is a plane on the runway(Which is) ready to take off.

Best regards,
Peter Parker

Directions: Respond to the email as if you are a new employee at Firme.com. In your email ask TWO questions and give 
ONE suggestion.

Trả lời:

			   - chỗ đỗ xe đạp cho nhân viên ở đâu?   Where employees can park their bikes?

--- Ý tưởng: 


Đề: Which do you prefer: e-books or books?
Give reasons or examples to support your opinion.

Giải: 


Mở bài: (Phải dẫn dắt phải lôi kéo người đọc, rồi mình mới nói thẳng để bài nó dài kkk)
In our technological era/ modern society, ebook have become more and more popular, especially among
young people. ( Mở bài chung các bài kkk) However, There are still many people who favor(prefer) 
traditional books. (Tới ý kiến của mình) Personally, I prefer using e-books and there
are many reason to support my opinion. 

Thân bài: (Đưa ra ví dụ, lý do để hỗ trợ cái ý kiến của mình) 
(lý do 1) 
First, it's more convenient. I don't have to go to the library or bookstore to buy e-books. 
They are available to download 24 hours of the day, any day of the year, from the comfort
of my home. (Lấy ví dụ) I live in an area where there are not many libraries and bookstores.
In the past, it usually took me 2 hours to go buy or borrow books. Since I switched to 
e-books, i have been able to spend those 2 hours on many other important things. 

Second, e-books are usually cheaper than paper books. This is because there are no costs
related to shipping and packaging. In fact/ As a matter of fact, e-books are often 30-50%
cheaper than their physical counterparts( những vật giá tương tự). For people who live on 
a tight budget, this is a huge benefit. 

Last but not least, the experience of using e-books are more pleasurable(=enjoyable). 
When I read e-books, I can adjust the text size, color or font style as I like. However,
I can't do those things with tradition books.(Ví dụ) I feel like my eyes have become less
tired after using e-books. Moreover, e-books can contain multimedia (đa phương tiện)
elements which are not available with paper books. These elements, such as audio or video, 
can make reading a fun and engaging experience(trải nghiệm hấp dẫn và vui). 

To sum up, I prefer e-books to paper books. ( Đủ nếu cần cao thì vẽ thêm kkk) 
I think people should choose the book format which suits their needs and preference.
