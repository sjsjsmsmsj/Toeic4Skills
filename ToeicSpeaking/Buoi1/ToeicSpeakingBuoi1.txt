	Do you think it's useful to check information about a 
	product on the company's website before buying it?

	- I don't think it's useful to check information product on 
	the company's website before buying it.

	- The main reason is that information on the 
	company's website is often incomplete. This is
	because the company give some good review and hide
	bad review on their website. Therefore, it's difficult
	for customers to have a complete understanding of the product.

	- Let me tell you my story. Last year, I bought a 
	smartphone after checking information about it on 
	the company's website. According to the description 
	on the website, it can take beautiful photos and it can 
	store many files. When I received the smartphone, it did take
	beautiful photos and it did store many files. However, it also 
	ran out of battery very quickly. This bad feature was not mentioned 
	on the website. That's why I think so.
